Modern MTG Tournament Rules

General Information

Format: Modern
Number of Players: 8

Tournament Structure

3.1. Swiss rounds.
3.2. Rounds will be best-of-three matches.

Proxy Guidelines

Proxy-Friendly: Proxies are allowed but must adhere to the following standards:

  • Proxies must be in good condition, legible, and professionally presented (printed or altered cards that resemble real cards).
  • Cards should be double-sleeved to protect the proxies and ensure uniformity (optional but recommended).
  • The back side of the sleeves must not be transparent to prevent any distinction between proxies and real cards.
  • Proxies must not stick out of the sleeves or cause any noticeable difference in deck thickness.

Deck Construction Rules

Decks must comply with Modern format legality:

  • Use only cards from sets legal in Modern.
  • Adhere to the Modern banned list.

Tournament Conduct

Sportsmanship: This is a friendly tournament. Players are expected to:

  • Be respectful to opponents and organizers.
  • Resolve disputes amicably.
  • Play at a reasonable pace.

Gameplay Rules: Follow the official Magic: The Gathering Comprehensive Rules:

  • Keep track of life totals clearly.
  • Clearly announce plays and intentions.

Additional Notes

Bring dice, tokens, and any other necessary accessories.
Players must ensure their decks are shuffled and randomized before each game.
